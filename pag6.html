Brie Toast:

This is a very easy toast to make, which will not take you more than 13 minutes, very delicious and healthy.

Preparatión time

  • Total: Approximately 13 minutes.
  • Preparation: 5 minutes.
  • Cooking: 8 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Cut the onion into thin strips.
  2. Melt the butter in a frying pan and cook the onion over low heat so it doesn't dehydrate.
  3. Toast the bread, add the brie cheese and top with the cooked onion and a few slices of tomato.
  4. Enjoy this amazing and delicious toast.

Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Bread cut into slices
  • Brie
  • Tomato slices (optional)
