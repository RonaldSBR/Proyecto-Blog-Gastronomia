Pumpkin soup

This is a pumpkin soup that is very easy to make and that you will love, healthy and quick to make.

Preparatión time

  • Total: Approximately 45 minutes.
  • Preparation: 15 minutes.
  • Cooking: 30 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Cut the squash into chunks and season with salt.
  2. Bake the pumpkin at 180°C until soft.
  3. Caramelize the onions over low heat with the butter.
  4. Fry the previously chopped garlic.
  5. Blend the pumpkin with the garlic and onion. Add a little water if necessary to get the consistency of cream.
  6. Garnish with sliced almonds.

Ingredients

  • 1 pumpkin
  • 2 onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Sliced almonds for garnish
