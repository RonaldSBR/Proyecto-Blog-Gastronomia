Vegetable soup and cubes:

This is a very easy recipe to make, healthy and with few ingredients.

Preparatión time

  • Total: Approximately 50 minutes.
  • Preparation: 20 minutes.
  • Cooking: 30 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Cut the vegetables into chunks and season them with salt.
  2. Cook the vegetables in vegetable broth until tender.
  3. Blend the vegetables with a blender until you get a smooth cream.
  4. Serve hot and add a drizzle of olive oil.
  5. Enjoy This Healthy Recipe.

Ingredients

  • Mixed vegetables (squash, carrot, zucchini, etc.)
  • Vegetable broth
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
