Apple tartlets:

This apple pie is perfect if you want to try something sweet and healthy, very easy to make in no time.

Preparatión time

  • Total: Approximately 35 minutes.
  • Preparation: 20 minutes.
  • Cooking: 15 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Cut the peeled apples into thin slices.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry, brush with butter and sprinkle with brown sugar.
  3. Place the apple on top of the stripped-up puff pastry and roll it into a flower shape.
  4. Bake at 180°C until the puff pastry is golden brown.
  5. Enjoy this delicious cake, you can accompany it with your favorite sweet sauce if you wish.

Ingredients

  • 3 apples
  • 250 g puff pastry
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
