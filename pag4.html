Baked salmon:

This recipe is very quick and easy to make, plus it's very healthy.

Preparatión time

  • Total: Approximately 25 minutes.
  • Preparation: 10 minutes.
  • Cooking: 15 minutes.

Instructions

  1. Grease a baking dish and place the salmon skin side down.
  2. Season with salt and pepper and add the dill and lemon zest.
  3. Bake at 180°C for 15 minutes.
  4. Add the capers before serving.

Ingredients

  • 500 g salmon
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon dill
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • Capers to taste
